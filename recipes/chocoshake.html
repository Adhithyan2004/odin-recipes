Chocoshake

chocolate milkshake Chocolate milk is made with milk and chocolate syrup (usually made with cocoa powder, sugar, and a bit of water)

a chocolate milkshake is made with milk, chocolate syrup, and ice cream, so it's much richer, thicker, and frostier.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Choco syrup
  • chocolate bars
  • Choco ice cream

Steps:

  1. Take 200ml of milk
  2. Add the choco syrup to the milk
  3. Add chocolate bars for extra sweetness
  4. Put a scoop of choco ice cream
  5. Your milkshake is ready to serve
